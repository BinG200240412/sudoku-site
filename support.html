常见问题

怎么玩？

选择一个空格，点下方数字填入。每一行、每一列、每个 3×3 宫内，1–9 各出现一次即胜。新手可在开场选择「我是新手」，会有真棋盘上的分步教程带你上手。

四档难度有什么区别？

简单 / 中等 / 困难 / 大师，逐级提升。大师级是世界公认的经典残局与分级题库，挑战极限。

AI 导师怎么用？

解题卡住时，导师会在棋盘上高亮演示下一步的逻辑（裸单、隐单、裸对、区块指向、X-Wing 等），教你怎么想，而不只是给答案。

「纸上模式」是什么？

像在报纸上解题：不限错误、不标红、不高亮，填满后才揭晓对错——对错全凭自己判断。可在开场选择，或在设置里开启。

我换了手机 / 重装了 App，购买怎么恢复？

用同一个 Apple ID，在解锁页点「恢复购买 / Restore」即可免费恢复。一次买断，永久有效。

我的数据安全吗？

非常安全——App 不收集、不上传任何数据，一切都只存在你的设备本地。详见 隐私政策。

FAQ

How do I play?

Tap an empty cell, then tap a number below to fill it. Fill the grid so every row, every column, and every 3×3 box contains 1–9 exactly once. New to sudoku? Choose "I'm new" at the start for a step-by-step tutorial right on the board.

What's the difference between the difficulties?

Easy / Medium / Hard / Master, rising in order. Master features world-renowned classic puzzles and a graded bank, for the very edge of the game.

How does the AI coach work?

Stuck? The coach highlights the logic of your next move right on the board — naked singles, hidden singles, naked pairs, pointing pairs, X-Wing and more — teaching you how to think, not just handing you the answer.

What is "Paper mode"?

Solve like it's newsprint: no mistake limit, nothing flagged, nothing highlighted. The verdict only appears once the grid is full — it's yours alone. Choose it at the start, or turn it on in Settings.

I got a new phone / reinstalled. How do I restore my purchase?

With the same Apple ID, tap "Restore" on the unlock screen to restore for free. Pay once, yours forever.

Is my data safe?

Completely — the app collects and uploads nothing; everything stays on your device. See the Privacy Policy.
