隐私政策.

最后更新：2026 年 6 月 12 日

sudoku 在设计之初就把隐私放在第一位。简单说一句：我们不收集你的任何数据。

我们不做什么

  • 不收集——App 不收集、不存储、不上传任何个人信息。
  • 不追踪——没有任何统计分析、广告标识符或第三方追踪工具。
  • 不联网传数据——你的对局进度、设置、记录与存档全部保存在你自己的设备本地，永不离开设备。
  • 不需要账号——无需注册、无需登录、无需邮箱即可使用全部功能。

关于购买

一次性解锁通过 Apple 的 App Store 完成。支付由 Apple 处理，我们看不到、也不会保存你的任何支付信息。 该交易适用 Apple 的隐私政策。

关于儿童

App 不向任何人收集数据，儿童亦然。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

sudoku was built privacy-first. In one line: we do not collect any of your data.

What we don't do

  • No collection — the app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • No tracking — no analytics, no advertising identifiers, no third-party trackers.
  • On-device only — your game progress, settings, records, and saved puzzles live locally on your device and never leave it.
  • No accounts — no sign-up, no login, no email required to use every feature.

Purchases

The one-time unlock is handled by Apple through the App Store. Payment is processed by Apple, and we never see or store your payment information. That transaction is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, children included.
